About VHCStore
🚴‍♂️

For over 25 years, VHCStore has been a trusted name in the world of bicycles, helping riders of all ages find the perfect ride. From humble beginnings to becoming a go-to destination for cycling enthusiasts, our journey has been built on passion, trust, and dedication to customer satisfaction.

📍 Our Stores & Branches

We proudly operate two branches in Thane and three in Mumbai, ensuring that cycling enthusiasts across both cities have access to the best bicycles and services.

  • ✅ Thane Branches (2) – Conveniently located for easy access.
  • ✅ Mumbai Branches (3) – Catering to cycling enthusiasts in the city.

🚴‍♀️ Why Choose VHCStore?

  • ✅ Full-Service Repairs: In-house maintenance & tune-ups.
  • ✅ Affordable Pricing: Best deals & discounts throughout the year.
  • ✅ Top Brands: High-quality cycles with durability & performance.
  • ✅ Expert Guidance: Personalized service to find your perfect cycle.
  • ✅ Wide Variety: Mountain bikes, road bikes, kids’ cycles, and more.

🌎 More Than Just a Store – A Cycling Community

We believe in promoting a healthy and eco-friendly lifestyle through cycling. That’s why we regularly organize:

  • 🚴 Group Rides & Events: Explore scenic routes with us!
  • 🏅 Cycling Competitions: We support amateur and professional cyclists.
  • 🔧 Workshops & Maintenance Camps: Learn how to take care of your cycle.
